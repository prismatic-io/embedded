## Customer Dashboard

A single pane for the signed-in customer covering integrations, instances, executions, and activity — drop it in as their home base instead of sending them to the Prismatic app. Rendered with `showDashboard()`.

Hide tabs you don't want customers to see via `screenConfiguration.dashboard.hideTabs`. This example hides **Attachments**.

Full reference: [Show Dashboard](https://prismatic.io/docs/embed/additional-screens/show-dashboard/).
