## Connections

Give customers one place to manage the reusable connections shared across their instances and workflows. From the connection detail screen, customers can see which instances and workflows use a connection, edit its credentials, or delete it. Rendered with `showConnections()`.

Full reference: [Connections Screen](https://prismatic.io/docs/embed/additional-screens/show-connections/). Background on reusable connections: [Reusable Connections](https://prismatic.io/docs/integrations/connections/integration-agnostic-connections/).
